_Flux_ is a measure of the amount of a vector field (such as electric or magnetic) passing through a given surface area==.

_Radiant flux_, also known as _power_, is the total amount of energy passing through a surface or region of space per unit time. Radiant flux can be found by taking the limit of differential energy per differential time:

$$\Phi=lim_{\Delta t\rightarrow0}\frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta t}=\frac{dQ}{dt}$$
> Q is [[Energy|energy]] in joules, t is time
